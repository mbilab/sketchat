We sketch. We chat.

You can quickly hold an online video chat and have a sketching board to help your discussion more efficient. It's 100% free, no installation and no registration required, just sign up a room and enjoy it.

How to Sketchat

It's quite simple and easy to make it.

Step 1: Sign-up a room

Create a chatroom entering your nickname and room name, or select a room created by your friends.

Step 2: Share the link

Share the url link with your friends! Up to 5 broadcast at once.

Step 3: Enjoy the chat

Allow the browser access to your camera and mic, and talk from anywhere in the world with video, audio chat or drawing.
